## Coverages and Benefits

### 1. OPD Expenses (Section II.1)
- **What is covered**:
  - Doctor Consultation (MBBS qualified practitioners)
  - Diagnostic Tests (listed in Policy Schedule)
  - Pharmacy Expenses (out-patient basis)
- **What is not covered**:
  - Out-patient consultations exceeding limits
  - Out-patient diagnostic tests exceeding limits
  - Out-patient pharmacy expenses exceeding limits
- **Exclusions**:
  - In-patient treatment and day care procedures
  - Naturopathy treatment(s)
  - CT & MRI

### 2. Cancer Secure Cover (Section II.2)
- **Option 1**:
  - Maximum liability: 100% of Sum Insured
  - Maximum number of claims: Only one claim for either Major Stage Cancer or Advanced Stage Cancer
  - Termination: After claim for Major Stage Cancer or Advanced Stage Cancer, or death, or end of policy period
- **Option 2**:
  - Maximum liability: 150% of Sum Insured
  - Maximum number of claims: One claim for Early Stage Cancer, one for Major Stage Cancer or Advanced Stage Cancer
  - Termination: After claim for Major Stage Cancer or Advanced Stage Cancer, or death, or end of policy period
- **Additional Benefits**:
  - Second Opinion (Section II.2.1)
  - Post Cancer OPD Consultations/Medication (Section II.2.2)
  - Counselling Sessions (Section II.2.3)
  - Reconstructive Surgery (Section II.2.4)
  - Palliative Care (Section II.2.5)
  - Genetic Testing (Section II.2.6)
- **Waiting Period**: Initial Waiting Period (applicable for both Options 1 and 2)
- **Survival Period**: Applicable for both Options 1 and 2

### 3. Income Protect (Section II.3)
- **What is covered**: Monthly limit for each continuous and completed month of hospitalization exceeding 7 consecutive days
- **Eligibility**: Salaried individuals with ITR evidence
- **Conditions**:
  - Deductible days specified in Policy Schedule
  - Maximum duration specified in Policy Schedule
  - Certification by treating Medical Practitioner
- **Exclusions**:
  - Pre-existing diseases within waiting period
  - Involuntary unemployment due to dishonesty, misconduct, fraud, poor performance
  - Voluntary unemployment, self-resignation, or voluntary retirement
  - Unemployment from casual, temporary, seasonal or contractual jobs
  - Involuntary unemployment due to probation period
  - Suspension due to pending enquiry
  - Unemployment due to maternity/paternity leave
  - Unemployment due to strike or labor disturbance
  - Unemployment due to withdrawal of employment offer

### 4. Preferred Provider Network (Section II.4)
- **What is covered**: Medical Expenses only at Network Providers
- **Conditions**:
  - Only Cashless facilities available
  - Not covered beyond Network Providers
  - Applicable only along with Section II.1 'OPD Expenses'

### 5. Heart Secure Cover (Section II.5)
- **Covered Illnesses**:
  1. First Heart Attack of specified severity
  2. Open Chest CABG
  3. Open Heart Replacement Or Repair Of Heart Valves
  4. Stroke Resulting In Permanent Symptoms
  5. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator
  6. Insertion of Pacemaker
  7. Angioplasty
  8. Minimally Invasive surgery of Aorta
  9. Major Surgery of Aorta
  10. Balloon Valvotomy or Valvuloplasty
  11. Heart Transplant
  12. Primary (Idiopathic) Pulmonary Hypertension
- **Conditions**:
  - Maximum liability: 100% of Sum Insured
  - Maximum number of claims: Only one claim for any covered illness
  - If more than one covered illness diagnosed within 48 hours, only one claim with highest benefit payout admissible
- **Waiting Period**: Initial Waiting Period (applicable for all 3 Options)
- **Survival Period**: Applicable for all 3 Options

### 6. Cancer Assure Cover (Section II.6)
- **Covered Treatments**:
  - Conventional treatments: Chemotherapy, Radiotherapy, Organ transplantation, Surgeries for excision of cancerous tissue
  - Advanced treatments: Immunotherapy, Personalised & Targeted therapy, Hormonal Therapy, Stem cell transplantation
- **In-patient Hospitalization**:
  - Room Rent (Single Private A/C Room category)
  - ICU Charges
  - Operation theatre expenses
  - Medical Practitioner's fees
  - Qualified Nurses charges
  - Medicines, drugs and other allowable consumables
  - Investigative tests or diagnostic procedures
  - Anaesthesia, blood, oxygen and blood transfusion charges
  - Surgical appliances and allowable prosthetic devices
  - Medication applied as per medical prescription
- **Day Care Treatment**:
  - Covered for Day Care Treatment related to covered illness
- **OPD Treatment**:
  - Covered for consultations, diagnostic tests, pharmacy expenses
- **Pre-hospitalization Medical Expenses**:
  - Covered for specified number of days before hospitalization
- **Post-hospitalization Medical Expenses**:
  - Covered for specified number of days after hospitalization
- **Emergency Road Ambulance Cover**:
  - Covered for transportation to nearest hospital
- **Follow Up Care Post Treatment**:
  - Covered for twice in a Policy Year
- **Exclusions**:
  - Non Allopathic treatment
  - Hospice care and palliative care
  - External appliances and devices used for diagnosis or treatment (except intra-operatively)
  - Prosthetic and other devices which are self-detachable/removable without surgery involving anaesthesia
  - Primary Hospitalization for treatment of Adjuvant Chemo / Supportive chemo & Oral Chemo

### 7. Hospital Cash Benefit (Section II.7)
- **What is covered**: Daily cash benefit for each continuous and completed 24 hours of Hospitalization
- **Conditions**:
  - Maximum number of days specified in Policy Schedule
  - Deductible as specified in Policy Schedule
- **Exclusions**:
  - Hospitalization as donor for another person's organ transplantation

### 8. Major Illnesses Cover - Domestic Hospitalization (Section II.8)
- **Covered Illnesses**:
  1. Cancer Treatment
  2. Coronary Artery By-Pass surgery
  3. Heart Valve Replacement
  4. Major Organ Transplantation
  5. Bone Marrow Transplant
  6. Neurosurgery
  7. Pulmonary artery graft surgery
  8. Aorta Graft Surgery
  9. Coronary Artery By-Pass surgery post occurrence of Myocardial Infraction
  10. Surgical treatment for Stroke
  11. Surgical treatment for benign Brain tumour
  12. Lung Transplant Surgery in case of End Stage Lung Disease
  13. Kidney Transplant Surgery in case of End Stage Renal Failure
  14. Skin grafting surgery for Major Burns
  15. Surgical treatment of Coma
  16. Surgery for Pheochromocytoma
- **In-patient Hospitalization**:
  - Room Rent (Single Private A/C Room category)
  1. ICU Charges
  2. Operation theatre expenses
  3. Medical Practitioner's fees
  4. Qualified Nurses charges
  5. Medicines, drugs and other allowable consumables
  6. Investigative tests or diagnostic procedures
  7. Anaesthesia, blood, oxygen and blood transfusion charges
  8. Surgical appliances and allowable prosthetic devices
  9. Medication applied as per medical prescription
- **Pre-hospitalization Medical Expenses**:
  - Covered for specified number of days before hospitalization
- **Post-hospitalization Medical Expenses**:
  - Covered for specified number of days after hospitalization
- **Emergency Road Ambulance Cover**:
  - Covered for transportation to nearest hospital
- **Waiting Period**: Initial Waiting Period (applicable for all covered illnesses)

### 9. Credit Protect (Section II.9)
- **What is covered**:
  - Accidental Death (AD): Amount commensurate with balance outstanding loan amount
  - Permanent Total Disablement (PTD): Amount commensurate with balance outstanding loan amount
- **Definitions**:
  - Accidental Death: Death within 365 days from date of accident
  - Permanent Total Disablement: Permanent total disablement with specific conditions
- **Exclusions**:
  - Consequential losses
  - Losses from motor racing or trial runs
  - Curative treatments performed against medical advice
