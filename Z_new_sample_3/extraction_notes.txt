5. Coverage Sections (benefits) – extracted details
   - **OPD Expenses (Section II.1)**
        *Covered*: Doctor consultation (including e‑consult & tele‑consult) for listed specialties, diagnostic tests, pharmacy expenses – all on an outpatient basis up to limits specified in the schedule (limits not present in markdown).
        *Not Covered*: Any outpatient consultation/diagnostic/pharmacy expense exceeding the schedule limits; inpatient treatment & day‑care procedures; Naturopathy treatments; CT & MRI.
   - **Cancer Secure Cover (Section II.2)**
        *Covered*: Percentage of Sum Insured payable for Early, Major, Advanced Stage Cancer as per selected option; claim terminates on death, claim admission, or policy expiry.
        *Conditions*: Waiting period (initial), survival period, claim limits (one claim per stage), grid of payouts.
        *Exclusions*: Illnesses not listed as covered, narcotics unless prescribed.
   - **Second Opinion (Section 2.1)** – one e‑opinion per covered cancer stage per policy period.
   - **Post‑Cancer OPD / Medication (Section 2.2)** – limit per insured per policy year for OPD consultations & medication after cancer claim.
   - **Counselling Sessions (Section 2.3)** – limit per policy year; only after certain cancer claims.
   - **Reconstructive Surgery (Section 2.4)** – limit per policy year.
   - **Palliative Care (Section 2.5)** – daily limit for a maximum period (numeric not provided).
   - **Genetic Testing (Section 2.6)** – limit per policy year.
   - **Income Protect (Section II.3)**
        *Covered*: Monthly income benefit for continuous disability >7 days, subject to eligibility (salaried, employment proof, deductible days, etc.).
        *Exclusions*: Various unemployment types, voluntary resignation, etc.
   - **Preferred Provider Network (Section II.4)** – cashless facility only at network providers; no coverage outside network.
   - **Heart Secure Cover (Section II.5)** – 100% liability for listed cardiac illnesses/surgeries; one claim per insured; waiting period & survival period apply.
   - **Heart Assure Cover (Section II.10)** – similar to Heart Secure but with detailed inpatient benefit list (room rent, ICU, OT, fees, medicines, investigations, etc.) – 100% liability.
   - **Cancer Assure Cover (Section II.6)** – covers conventional (chemo, radiotherapy, surgery, transplantation) and advanced treatments (immunotherapy, targeted therapy, hormonal, stem‑cell); percentages not specified.
   - **Hospital Cash Benefit (Section II.7)** – daily cash benefit per continuous 24‑hour hospitalization, subject to maximum days and deductible.
   - **Major Illnesses Cover – Domestic Hospitalization (Section II.8)** – covers a long list of major illnesses (cancer, coronary bypass, valve replacement, organ transplant, etc.) with 100% liability; includes pre‑, post‑hospitalization, ambulance, waiting & survival periods.
   - **Credit Protect (Section II.9)** – pays outstanding loan balance on accidental death or permanent total disablement, subject to limits.
